About Kodiak Electric, LLC - Your Local Tacoma Commercial Electrician

Kodiak Electric, LLC is a full service Tacoma commercial electrician and industrial electrician with a commitment to customer satisfaction and quality workmanship.  This message was established when we first opened our doors and continues today….on every job, with every customer.  What that means is that we take great pride in our commitment to your complete satisfaction.


Our commercial and industrial projects vary greatly, but have included: 

- Clinics

- Schools

- Health Facilities

- Tacoma School Districts 

We are also a registered TERO contractor (Tribal Employment Rights Office).

One of Kodiak Electric, LLC’s greatest strengths is our longevity.  Years of experience in the industry business has provided us with knowledge and expertise in a wide range of Tacoma electrical services, and is why so many clients have trusted us to be their single source contractor for all their industry needs. 

Our experience and involvement in the electrical industry has helped build and maintain partnerships with numerous local leaders; so you can trust the advice we give and the work we do!  More than just your average contractor, our clients view us as a knowledgeable and expert resource. They regularly engage us early in the planning and design stage recognizing our unique ability to identify the right fit for their specific project and budget.

We service all types of clients with complete knowledge and expertise. Our experience and precision craftsmanship set us apart from our competition. Our Tacoma electricians are truly profressional. They stay up to date with the latest electric codes, technology and techniques. We offer a wide range of services including tenant improvements, LED lighting, lighting analysis, facility upgrades, design lighting, design build, remodels, recessed lighting, new construction, retrofits, parking lot lighting, and much more.

Kodiak Electric, LLC is committed to ensuring that our customers receive the highest quality service with professionalism and attention to detail. We take care of each client’s needs from start to finish and are accessible and available around the clock to address any questions or concerns. Our goal is to provide quality work that will last, excellent customer service and communication throughout your entire electrical project and finish on time and within budget. We offer upfront pricing, no hidden fees and will explain everything in detail so that you understand what is happening throughout the entire project.

Our hard work, compassion and quality workmanship are the reasons for our success today, tomorrow and in the future. We look forward to serving all of your electrical needs.
