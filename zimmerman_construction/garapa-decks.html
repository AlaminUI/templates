Garapa Decks by Zimmerman Construction


Zimmerman Construction offers Garapa decking. Garapa is an exotic hardwood that is virtually identical to Ipe hardwood, but less expensive. It's the perfect alternative, looking just like Ipe and being just as durable. Garapa decking is a great decking material option to provide an all natural real wood deck surface that is just right for you, without breaking the budget.

Garapa is one of the finest quality hardwoods available and features a fine-grained timber that is light yellow to a warm golden hue and features the following:

1. Naturally resistant to rot, decay and insect attack.
2. Naturally scratch resistant which allows the surface to remain smooth for many years.
3. 30+ year lifespan without preservatives!
4. Responsibly harvested from managed forest to provide a truly renewable resource. 
5. Virtually identical to Ipe and is available at a fraction of the cost.
