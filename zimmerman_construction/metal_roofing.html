Metal roofing - standing seam metal roofing


Zimmerman Construction combines the strength of steel roofing with professional, expert roofing services. Our durable, beautiful and sustainable metal roofing solutions will add value to your home as they protect you and your family for a lifetime. Best of all, our commitment to complete, quality service from estimate to installation and the support of a great warranty give you peace of mind. We’ve got you covered.

Our roofs are covered by manufacturer 40 YR paint, chip, and fade warranties. Stone coated metal roof styles even have lifetime warranties unlimited transference to new homeowners backed by the manufacturer.
