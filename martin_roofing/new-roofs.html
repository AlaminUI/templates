New Roofs

Having good tires is essential for your safety, so you need to make sure you always purchase the right tires for your vehicle and driving conditions. Tires have a great impact on your ability to control your vehicle, and good tires allow for quick stops and adequate friction with the road surface.

From regular highway tires to winter tires, you can find all the types you need at New Roofs verbiage and have them delivered straight to your home. Take advantage of our unique wholesale pricing and remain safe on the road, without breaking the bank.

If you know your tires are worn or unsatisfactory, it would be wise to consider purchasing new tires through New Roofs verbiage. When tires are worn, they won’t perform correctly. Worn tires heat up more quickly, puncture more easily, and take on roads less effectively. They may also emerge as a potential danger to their driver and other motorists.

Manufacturers recommend tires must not be used for longer than 10 years. Replacing tires used to be suggested about every 5 years, yet with regular usage and hot summer temperatures, tires are now being replaced every 2 years. Wear happens with regular driving. Other factors such as driving habits, roadway conditions and weather may reduce the life of your tires.

You need new tires if:

• Your tread is below 4/32, or can not pass the "penny" test.
• Footstep is noticeably minimized or flush with the tread bars.
• Irregular tread wear.
• Bulges, bumps or soft spots on the tire.
• Damages to the tire that can not be fixed.
