About Us


Garen-T Cleaning Specialists LLC has been providing superior commercial cleaning services to the island of Oahu since 1998, a member of the Better Business Bureau and listed in the Department of Commerce and Consumer Affairs. We are licensed and fully insured for your protection.

We are a locally owned full-service commercial cleaning contractor, dedicated to the evaluation, cleaning and maintenance of your facility. Our professional staff is qualified to evaluate your needs and will assist you in making the appropriate choice of materials and applications.

We service our commercial cleaning clients with complete knowledge and expertise. Our experience and attention to detail set us apart from our competition.
