Fairbanks Electrical Safety Inspections

When buying a new home it is important to inspect for old, outdated, faulty or improper wiring. You should always know the condition of the wiring and the electrical system of any Fairbanks home before purchasing it.

Bright Electric will thoroughly inspect the electrical on your home and ensure that it is up to Fairbanks electrical code compliance and to also make sure it is safe for you and your family. If your home or office is not up to standard, our Fairbanks electricians can make any corrections needed to bring it up to code.

At Bright Electric, we want you to feel safe and trust the electrical system in your home. Last thing you need is an electrical fire or accident damaging your home or belongings. Our Fairbanks electricians are focused on getting the job done right the first time. We are your #1 Fairbanks electrical inspection expert...that you can trust.
